How does [...] paint the city?

The main goal of this visualization is to draw the city through activities that citizens do on a daily basis: living, breathing, buying food, cycling and buying clothes. The representation strategy depicts the contrasts within the city bounds depending on the theme analyzed. Where do people live, which zones have cleaner air, where are the clothing stores located, where does commerce have a strong presence and which areas offer better cycling conditions.
